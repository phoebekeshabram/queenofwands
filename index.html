Discernment

Is This Work For You?

I intentionally work with a small number of clients at a time. This is not for everyone — and that is by design.

You are a fit if you are...
  • Ready to look honestly at your patterns
  • Committed to your own growth, not just curious
  • Willing to sit with discomfort
  • Open to astrology and tarot as real tools
  • Taking full responsibility for your life
  • Serious about shadow integration
This may not be the space if you...
  • Want someone to tell you what you want to hear
  • Are seeking a replacement for therapy
  • Expect magic without personal work
  • Are not ready to examine your own role
  • Want certainty instead of clarity
  • Are in active crisis (please seek professional support)
